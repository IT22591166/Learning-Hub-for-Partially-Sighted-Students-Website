Description Show improved integration and evaluation results, with refined personalization and learning activities.

Research Logbook Due: March 2026 Marks: 2%
Description Maintain an up-to-date log of research activities, meeting notes, reading summaries, and experiment outcomes.

Research Paper Due: May 2026 Marks: 10%
Description Submit the research paper describing the problem, approach, methodology, results, and discussion.
Focus Clear writing, proper citations, experimental results, and a concise explanation of the contribution.
Final Presentation and Viva Due: May 2026 Marks: 40% + 20%
Description Final system presentation and demonstration, documentation submission, and performance evaluation.
Deliverables Final report, implementation details, test results, user guide, and presentation slides.
Viva Oral examination covering research contribution, technical choices, and evaluation results.
Preparation Be ready to justify methodology, show evidence of testing, and discuss limitations/future work.
